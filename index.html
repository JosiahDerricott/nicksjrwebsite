Our Story

We are a gourmet burger and gyro restaurant. We use 100% fresh ground beef, hand-made daily (never frozen!) and char-broiled to perfection. We offer a variety of specialty burgers with a twist, plus classic favorites and gyros as well!

With over three decades of restaurant experience, including the famous Zorba's Pizza in Mukilteo and the classic McGrath's in Ballard, Nick's Jr. is the latest restaurant from restaurateur Nick Demonakos.
